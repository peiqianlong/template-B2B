About Us

Guangdong Xinyuan Color Printing Co., Ltd located in South China's printing center —— Guangzhou, is  high-end and modern printing enterprise directly under China Post with a registered capital of RMB60 million and total assets of over RMB100 million.   Xinyuan currently has approximatedly 23000 ㎡ of workshop and office land and 3000 ㎡  of warehousing land as well as 560 technical professionals and high quality skilled workers.
